Max Verstappen is a Dutch racing driver currently competing in Formula One with Red Bull Racing. He is the son of former Formula One driver Jos Verstappen. Max became the youngest driver to compete in Formula One at the age of 17 years and 166 days during the 2015 Australian Grand Prix. He has since become one of the most successful drivers in the sport, winning multiple races and championships.

Scuderia Ferrari is the racing team division of the Ferrari automobile marque. Founded by Enzo Ferrari, the team has competed in Formula One since the inaugural season in 1950. Ferrari is the oldest and most successful team in the history of the sport, having won numerous Constructors' and Drivers' Championships. The team is known for its passionate fan base, often referred to as the "Tifosi," and its iconic red cars. Over the years, Ferrari has been home to many legendary drivers, including Michael Schumacher, who won five consecutive World Championships with the team.
